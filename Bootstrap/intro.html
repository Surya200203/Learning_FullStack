Bootstrap is a powerful, feature-packed forntend toolkit. Build anything from prototype to production in minutes.

Benefits of Bootstrap

Container

Containers are a fundamental building block of Bootstrap that contain, pad, and align your content within a given device or viewport.Containers are used to contain, pad, and (sometimes) center the content within them.
