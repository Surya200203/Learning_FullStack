HTML Entities

  • An HTML entity is a peice of text ("string") that begins with an ampersand (&) and ends with a semicolon (;) .
  • Used to displayb reversed characters (which would otherwise interpreted as HTML code),and Invisible characters(like non breaking spaces).
  • Can also used in place of characters that are difficult to type with a standard keyboard.
  • Browser interprets them & renders correct character.
